Tissue Engineering Services

01

Skin Tissue Equivalents

Full-thickness skin models with keratinocyte epidermis over dermal fibroblast-laden collagen. Barrier function validated by TEER measurement. Used for cosmetic ingredient safety testing and wound healing research.

02

Cartilage & Fibrocartilage

Chondrocyte-laden GelMA scaffolds with zonal porosity mimicking articular cartilage structure. Meniscus and intervertebral disc models with fibrocartilage-to-hyaline gradients for orthopedic research and repair.

03

Vascularized Constructs

Perfusable vascular channels lined with endothelial cells for nutrient delivery to thick tissue constructs. HUVEC-seeded conduits from 200µm diameter using sacrificial Pluronic templates or coaxial printing.

BIOINK PLATFORM

Tissue-Specific Bioink Library

Every tissue type demands a specific bioink. Our formulation library covers the mechanical range from brain (0.1 kPa) to bone (GPa) with matched biological signals.

  • ● Type I collagen — skin dermis and tendon constructs
  • ● GelMA-HA — cartilage zone-specific compositions
  • ● Fibrin — vascular and cardiac tissue applications
  • ● Decellularized ECM — organ-specific matrix signals
  • ● PEG-based hydrogels — long-term culture stability
  • ● Custom co-culture and tri-culture bioink formulations
