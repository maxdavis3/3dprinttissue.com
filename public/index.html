Bioink Platform

Tissue-Specific Bioink Library

Every tissue type demands a specific bioink. Our formulation library covers the mechanical range from brain (0.1 kPa) to bone (GPa) with matched biological signals and cell-compatible crosslinking chemistry.

From Type I collagen for dermal constructs to decellularized ECM bioinks for organ-specific matrix signals — we match the material to the biology.

Research Fields

Transforming Medicine Across Sectors

Pharma & Toxicology Testing

Human-relevant tissue models that outperform animal testing for ADME, cytotoxicity, and efficacy screening. Reduce late-stage trial failures.

Wound Healing & Skin Grafts

Full-thickness skin equivalents with stratified epidermis over collagen-fibroblast dermis. Validated for cosmetic, wound healing, and burn research.

Orthopedic & Cartilage Repair

Chondrocyte-laden GelMA scaffolds with zonal porosity mimicking articular cartilage. Meniscus and IVD models for orthopedic R&D.

Ophthalmology & Corneal Models

Corneal stroma constructs with keratocyte-seeded collagen matrices for vision restoration research and ocular drug testing.
