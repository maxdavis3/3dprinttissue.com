About 3D Print Tissue

Our Story

Bioprinting at the Edge of Medicine

3D Print Tissue is the authoritative resource for bioprinting and tissue engineering — covering the science, technology, and clinical translation of additive biomanufacturing.

We combine deep materials science expertise with hands-on bioprinting experience across extrusion-based, inkjet, laser-assisted, and stereolithographic platforms to deliver tissue constructs that push the boundaries of regenerative medicine.

From custom bioink formulation to ISO 13485-compatible quality systems, we support pharmaceutical companies, academic labs, and medical device developers at every stage.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Cell-First Design

Every construct starts with the biology. We select bioinks, crosslinking chemistry, and printing parameters around cell survival and function — not the other way around.

Research-Grade Quality

Constructs are validated for cell viability (>85%), structural integrity, and reproducibility. Full documentation available for GLP and ISO 13485 environments.

Clinical Relevance

We prioritize physiologically relevant architectures — vascular hierarchies, zonal tissue gradients, and co-culture systems that recapitulate in vivo biology.
