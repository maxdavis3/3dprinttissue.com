process:
  - step: "01"
    title: "Consultation & Design"
    text: "We begin with your biological target — tissue type, cell source, intended assay. We select the bioink system, print architecture, and crosslinking strategy matched to your application."
  - step: "02"
    title: "Bioink Formulation"
    text: "Custom bioink preparation from our library or your specified formulation. Rheological characterization, sterility testing, and cell compatibility validation before printing."
  - step: "03"
    title: "Bioprinting"
    text: "Multi-material extrusion or stereolithographic printing in a biosafety cabinet. Process parameters optimized for maximum cell viability and construct fidelity."
  - step: "04"
    title: "Maturation & Culture"
    text: "Post-print culture in tissue-specific media with mechanical or biochemical stimulation as required. Bioreactor conditioning available for vascularized and load-bearing constructs."
  - step: "05"
    title: "Validation & Delivery"
    text: "Viability assessment (Live/Dead, Alamar Blue), structural imaging (confocal, µCT), and functional assays (TEER, contractility) before delivery with full documentation."
